About us

SIMlogical is a product developed by students at Fatima Jinnah Women University. It aims to provide a platform where circuits, Boolean Expression and Truth Table can be easily converted into each other using a graphical user interface. Our goal is to provide a interface that is simple, friendly and convenient. We are passionate about the latest technologies and hope to provide you with best experience using ever-improving technologies
